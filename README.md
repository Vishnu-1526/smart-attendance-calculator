# 📌 Smart Attendance Calculator

A **smart and simple attendance management app** designed for students.  
This app helps students calculate their current attendance percentage, track subject-wise attendance, and get insights on how many classes they can miss or need to attend to meet their target.

---

## 🚀 Features
- 📊 **Automatic Attendance Calculation** – Enter total classes and attended classes to get your percentage.  
- 📌 **Target Tracking** – Set your minimum attendance requirement (e.g., 75%) and know how many classes you can miss or must attend.  
- 🗂️ **Subject-wise Management** – Manage multiple subjects separately.  
- 🔔 **Reminders & Alerts** – Get alerts when attendance is at risk (optional).  
- 📱 **User-friendly UI** – Simple, clean, and easy to use.  
- 📈 **Predictions & Analysis** – Provides future attendance predictions and detailed analysis.  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React Native / Flutter / Android (depending on your build)  
- **Backend**: Firebase / Node.js / Local Storage (as per your setup)  
- **Database**: Firestore / SQLite / MySQL (customizable)  

---

## 📖 How It Works
1. Add your **subjects** and set your **attendance target**.  
2. Input the **number of classes attended** and **total classes conducted**.  
3. The app calculates your **attendance percentage** instantly.  
4. Get smart recommendations:  
   - ✅ How many more classes you can skip.  
   - 📈 How many classes you must attend to reach your target.  
   - 🔮 Predictions and analytics to plan your attendance better.
